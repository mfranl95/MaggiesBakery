About Maggie's Bakery

Maggie's Bakery is a family-owned bakery located in Greenwich Village. What makes Maggie's Bakery different from other bakeries in the New York Area is our willingness to be creative with our recipes. Our motto is "Nourish Your Sweet Side" because our delectable pastries will leave our customers 100% satisfied.
